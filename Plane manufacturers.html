Airbus has made the worlds largest passanger airplane and the worlds most eco friendly plane, the Airbus Beluga. Boeing

Boeing is the worlds largest and oldest aircraft manufacturer. It has made 20,000 planes in it's life time and has set a set of unbeatable world records like the worlds largest aircraft producer and the millitary transport plane. Boeing doesn't only make planes but satelites and has contributed by giving parts to the Mars Rover and even the fisrt rocket to land on the moon. Here is a link to the Official Boeing Website.

Antonov

Antonov is the worlds best cargo plane manufacturer and has broke so much world records. Anotov is a Ukrainian Plane Manufacturer that was made in 1938 by the Goverment of the Soviet Union. The company has the worlds largest plane and cargo plane and the worlds largest cargo plane to reach the highest altitude. Antonov is it's own transport company and doesn't sell most of it's planes to other companies. A plane that Anotov created is still used by Nasa, even though bought in 2008.
