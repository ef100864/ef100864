All about Planes

What are planes?

A plane is a type of vehicle used in everyday life. Planes are the only form of public transportation that can travel across countries and continents quickly. They serve various purposes: some are designed for transporting cargo, others for passenger travel, and many are used for military operations. These planes are modified according to their specific roles. The image above shows the Airbus A380, the world's largest passenger plane.

Different types of Airlines

An airline is a transportation business that facilitates travel to various destinations. Some airlines operate domestic flights (within a country), while others offer international services (between countries). Many airlines provide both!
