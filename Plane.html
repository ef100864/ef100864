The types of planes

Cargo Planes

A Cargo plane is a plane used for shipping stuff like products.These planes are owned by shipping companies like DHL and FedEx. The difference of cargo planes and other planes is that these planes wings alwas are at the top for long distance flight time. The worlds largest cargo plane ( it is also the worlds largest plane) is the AN225 with a total wingspan being larger that a football pitch.

Passenger Planes

A Passenger plane is used to transport people and these planes have numerous features to keep you and I safe and comfy. These planes are owned by Airlines which operate these. The difference of passanger planes and other types of planes are that the wings are on the bottom to provide a comfy and safe ride. The worlds largest passenger airplane is the Airbus A380.

Millitary planes

Millitary planes are used by the millitary and thats how they protect the country. Millitary Planes have a certain design to them which helps them on their missions. Millitary planes always have their wings in the middle to ensure a fast plane. To operate these, pilots have a special lisence because of their weapons and each and every millitary plane belongs to a nations millitary, Specifically the air force.
